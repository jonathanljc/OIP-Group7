Storyboarding

User Journey

We began our prototyping process with comprehensive storyboarding to map out user journeys and identify key interaction points. This helped us understand the complete visitor experience and design touchpoints that would seamlessly integrate into their natural flow.

Critical Discovery: Check-Out Challenge

Storyboarding revealed that users might forget to check out after their visit. This poses a risk to exit data accuracy, which is critical for planning and budgeting.

To address this, and inspired by positive user attitudes toward digital features during our user interviews, we introduced an Augmented Reality (AR) component to boost engagement while subtly reminding users to check out.
