Project Reflection

Insights gained, challenges overcome, and lessons learned

Key Insights

Discovered that 40% of visitors are elderly or families with young children who prefer physical interfaces over mobile apps. This led us to develop a dual-approach solution.

Challenges Faced

Balancing modern technology with accessibility requirements. Ensuring data privacy compliance while maintaining seamless user experience across different age groups.

Evolution of Understanding

Initially focused solely on mobile solutions, but stakeholder feedback revealed the need for inclusive design. This shifted our approach to a hybrid system.

Stakeholder Learnings

Regular feedback sessions with Kibble Palace staff revealed operational constraints and visitor behavior patterns that significantly influenced our design decisions.
